UV-C Cleaning Technology

EcoSip uses advanced UV-C light to disinfect your water and bottle. UV-C light penetrates and disrupts the DNA of microorganisms, killing 99.9% of bacteria and viruses. With a one-touch activation, you can enjoy fresh, clean water anytime without chemicals or filters. Stay healthy and hydrated with peace of mind.

Our companion app enhances your hydration experience. Track your daily water intake, set personalized hydration goals, and receive timely reminders to drink water. The app also monitors your EcoSip bottle’s cleaning cycles and battery life, ensuring you stay on top of your wellness routine with ease and efficiency.
